Research Project

"How does founders educational background impact startups funding?". This research paper investigates different factors of the education of founder to determine whether there is a connection to the amount of funding startups received. Data Analysis and Machine Learning techniques such as Linear Regression and Random Forrest were utlized to assess the importance of each factor. The main findings are that higher educational qualifications, STEM education and top institutions positively impact startup funding.
